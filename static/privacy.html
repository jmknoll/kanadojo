Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2025

The short version: Kana Flow collects no personal information. All data stays on your device. We don't track you, sell data, or use third-party analytics.

1. Overview

Kana Flow ("the app," "we," "us") is a Japanese kana learning application developed for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

By using Kana Flow, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Kana Flow does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • We do not use analytics services or crash-reporting tools that transmit data externally.
  • We do not require an account or sign-in of any kind.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Quiz progress — correct and incorrect answer counts per kana character.
  • App preferences — settings you configure within the app.

This data is stored using Apple's on-device frameworks (SwiftData) and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server or third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Kana Flow does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no third-party services that can access your data through this app.

5. Children's Privacy

Kana Flow does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by Kana Flow resides solely on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Using the Reset Progress option in the app's Settings screen.
  • Deleting the Kana Flow app from your device, which removes all associated data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

support@kanaflow.app
